What are the best beaches near Herceg Novi?
Top 3 beaches:
1. Zhanjice — crystal clear water, boat access
2. Mirista — hidden gem near a monastery
3. Savina — family-friendly, easy to reach
How do I get to Zhanjice?
Take a boat from Herceg Novi marina (15 min, ~5€) or drive via Lustica peninsula (30 min). Boats run every 30 min in summer.
Any restaurants nearby?
Recommended: Ribarsko Selo — fresh seafood right on the beach. Try the grilled octopus!
